Help Center

How do I create a meeting?

Sign in → Click Host → Fill title & duration → pick a scene → share the 8-letter code.

How do I join a meeting?

Click Meet → paste the code → hit Join.

What is Ready-Player-Me?

A free avatar service. Press Create Avatar on your profile, design, then export—your character is saved automatically.

Can I use my own Unity scene?

Yes. Replace the scene file in the Unity project and update the scene field via the API.

Dark mode?

Click the toggle icon in the top-right—your choice is remembered.
