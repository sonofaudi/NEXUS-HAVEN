About

Nexus Haven is an open-source, privacy-first virtual meeting platform that runs in your browser—No Installs, No tracking, Just human connection.

How Does It work?...

  1. Create an account in 5 seconds.
  2. Host or paste a meeting code.
  3. Pick a scene: conference room, jungle, classroom…
  4. Jump into a shared VR space with your Ready-Player-Me avatar.

Open Tech

Front-end: vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with CSS variables for instant dark/light mode.
Back-end: Node + Express + MongoDB + JWT.
VR: Unity loads avatars via protected HTTPS API.
